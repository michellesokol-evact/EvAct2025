NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. A PURCHASE DOES NOT INCREASE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED OR RESTRICTED BY LAW.

1. Eligibility

The Driving Greatness Contest ("Contest") is open to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States and the District of Columbia who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry. Nominators must be individuals (not businesses or organizations). Nominees must be current high school football coaches actively involved in high school athletic programs within the United States. Employees, officers, and directors of the Sponsor (defined below), its affiliates, subsidiaries, advertising and promotion agencies, and immediate family members (spouse, parent, child, sibling, and their respective household members) are not eligible to participate or win.

This Contest is sponsored by CharterUP, LLC ("Sponsor"). The Contest is governed by the laws of the State of Georgia.

3. Contest Period

The Contest begins on October 13, 2025, at 12:00 a.m. Central Time (CT) and ends on November 14, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. CT ("Contest Period"). Entries submitted before or after the Contest Period will not be eligible. Entries received outside of this period will not be accepted.

4. How to Enter

During the Contest Period, visit charterup.com/driving-greatness and complete the official nomination form, including: Your contact information; The nominated coach's information; A written description explaining why the coach demonstrates exceptional leadership and community impact. Optional: Upload a photo or video showcasing the coach's leadership. No purchase or payment of any kind is necessary to enter or win.

All submissions must: Be original and created by the entrant; Not contain any offensive, defamatory, or inappropriate content; Not infringe upon any third-party rights (including copyright, trademark, or privacy rights). 6. Judging Criteria

All eligible entries will be reviewed by a panel of judges selected by the Sponsor. The winner will be chosen based on the following criteria: Demonstrated leadership and positive influence (40%); Community impact (40%); Quality and clarity of nomination story (20%). The winner will be selected on or about November 14, 2025.

7. Prize

One (1) winning coach will receive: $10,000 awarded directly to the coach; and $10,000 awarded to the coach's team or school program in CharterUP credit, which is to be used on team transportation. Approximate Retail Value (ARV): $20,000 total.
