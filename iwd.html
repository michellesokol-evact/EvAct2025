Safe Water & Gender Equity

safe water is a women's equity issue

Women and girls spend an estimated 200 million hours every single day collecting water. That's 22,800 years of time—every day—lost to a task that falls overwhelmingly on them.

This isn't just a water problem. It's time stolen from school, from work, from rest, from building a future. And 1 in 3 schools lack the toilets girls need once they hit puberty, creating yet another barrier.

Evidence Action's safe water programs help remove this burden—giving women and girls back their time and protecting their families' health.
